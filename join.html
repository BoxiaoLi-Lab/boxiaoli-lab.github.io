Why Choose Us

We are highly motivated to push the boundaries of materials science. Our laboratory is strongly supported by the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) and major corporate projects in the automotive sector. We offer a highly collaborative environment, state-of-the-art facilities (including 2D-IR spectroscopy), and comprehensive mentorship. Whether you are passionate about fundamental polymer synthesis or industrial applications like flexible ionic sensors, you will find a platform to thrive here.
